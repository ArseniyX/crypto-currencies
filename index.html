This project about crypto currencies, you have ability:

  • In the main page you see the top 100 largest cryptocurrencies
  • If you press the "More" button, you will see an image and the current price of the cryptocurrency you choose in dollars, euros and shekels
  • You can switch to smart search, to find by name and crypto symbol name
  • You also have a chart, where you can see the price difference. To use it, click on toggle button to select the currency you are interested in and click the chart button
